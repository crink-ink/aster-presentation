Anatomy of a Path

Every Path is built from six kinds of elements.

A blend of reflection, conversation, and learning — mixed together so progress feels alive, not academic. At the center, Cri, our AI coach, personalizes everything to the individual.

i.

Assessments

Reflective questions that surface where the user stands — and what they're ready for next.

ii.

Chat with Cri

Our AI coach — clinically guided, judgment-free, and personalized to every user. Cri remembers, adapts, and walks alongside.

iii.

Mini reads

Short, evidence-based reads — written by clinicians, in the voice of a friend.

iv.

Action steps

Small commitments to practice in real life — completed between sessions to turn insight into habit.

v.

Flashcards

Bite-sized reminders — concept, prompt, takeaway. Easy to revisit, easy to save.

vi.

Video

Short clinician-led demonstrations — see the practice before you try it yourself.

AI
Powered by Cri AI

One Cri.
A different
Cri for everyone.

Cri isn't a chatbot bolted onto a course. It's a clinically-supervised AI that quietly learns the person behind the screen — their triggers, their language, their progress — and shapes every element around them.

  • Memory across the Path — Cri carries what it learns in Level 1 into Level 4
  • Adaptive tone — gentler when the user is struggling, sharper when they're flying
  • Clinically supervised — every checklist authored by Crink's therapists
  • Same Path, different journey — two users finish the same Path with two different stories
